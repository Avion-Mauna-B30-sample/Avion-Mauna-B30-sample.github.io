Manny Pacquiao

8-Division World Champion

Pound for Pound Fighter

BoxRec ranks Pacquiao as the third greatest boxer, pound for pound, of all time, as well as the greatest Asian boxer of all time. Boxing historian Bert Sugar ranked Pacquiao as the greatest southpaw fighter of all time.

The Fighting Pride of the Philippines

Manny Pacquiao, hailed as "The Fighting Pride of the Philippines," stands as a legendary figure in the world of boxing and a national hero to his fellow Filipinos. His relentless dedication, lightning-fast footwork, and explosive punching power have led him to conquer multiple weight divisions and claim numerous world titles. Manny Pacquiao's legacy transcends the boxing ring, leaving an indelible mark on the hearts of millions, as he continues to inspire generations with his passion, determination, and unwavering commitment to his country and the sport he loves.
